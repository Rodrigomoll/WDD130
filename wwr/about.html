About Montaña Perú

Your Trusted Adventure Partner Since 2010

Our Mission

At Montaña Perú, our mission goes beyond providing thrilling adventures. We are committed to:

• Creating safe and memorable experiences that challenge our guests to step outside their comfort zones

• Preserving and protecting the natural environments we explore

• Supporting local communities and promoting sustainable tourism

• Training the next generation of rafting enthusiasts and environmental stewards

Through every expedition we lead, we strive to maintain the perfect balance between adventure and environmental responsibility.

Our Expert Team

Our team consists of passionate outdoor enthusiasts with extensive experience in white water rafting and wilderness exploration. Each guide:

• Holds international rafting certifications

• Is trained in wilderness first aid and swift water rescue

• Has at least 5 years of professional guiding experience

• Speaks multiple languages to better serve our international guests

Many of our guides have competed in international rafting competitions and have led expeditions worldwide.

Our Values & Commitment

Safety is our top priority, but it's just the beginning of what we stand for:

• We maintain the highest safety standards in the industry

• Our equipment is regularly inspected and updated

• We contribute 5% of our profits to river conservation efforts

• We partner with local communities to provide employment and education

Every adventure with us is an opportunity to learn about and connect with nature while ensuring its preservation for future generations.
